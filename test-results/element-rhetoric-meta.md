## The Rhetorical Device

The paper uses itself as a demonstration of the AI displacement risk it models: an AI-written paper about AI displacing labor, including academic labor. This is the "meta" device.

## Element-by-Element Evaluation

### 1. Is the rhetorical device used in both the abstract and introduction? PASS

**Abstract (line 32):** "The production of this paper illustrates the mechanism it models: AI agents produced all analysis and writing from a human-authored specification, requiring zero traditional research labor."

**Introduction (lines 59--60):** "The displacement risk we model is not confined to asset prices and policy---it extends to the nature of productive work itself, including the work of producing research like this paper. The production of this paper offers an illustration. All analysis, code, and prose were produced by AI agents; the human author contributed only the economic specification and the test scripts that evaluate the output."

Both instances are present and substantive.

### 2. Would humans be turned off by the use of the rhetorical device? PASS (no, they would not be turned off)

The device is framed as an economic illustration of the model's mechanism, not as a boast about AI capabilities. This is the crucial design choice. The abstract sentence reads as a data point supporting the paper's thesis---"here is a concrete instance of displacement"---rather than as "look, AI wrote this." The introduction paragraph ties the device directly to the economic argument about displacement extending to knowledge production.

Given the arxiv rejection history (likely triggered by overt AI authorship signals), the current framing is well-calibrated. It acknowledges AI authorship transparently while giving it intellectual purpose. A human referee reading this would encounter the device as part of the argument, not as a provocation.

One mild risk: the footnote on line 59 repeats nearly the same content as the main text ("The human author wrote the economic specification and automated test scripts. AI agents produced all other content---derivations, code, figures, and prose---with no human editing applied."). This double-stating could read as slightly insistent. But the footnote serves a legitimate purpose---providing the precise division of labor---so this is acceptable.

### 3. Is the use of the rhetorical device compelling and interesting? PASS

The device is genuinely clever. A paper about AI displacing labor that is itself produced by AI is a natural and powerful illustration. It transforms what could be a liability (AI authorship) into a feature of the argument. The abstract's phrasing---"illustrates the mechanism it models"---is economical and precise. The introduction's framing---displacement extends to "the nature of productive work itself, including the work of producing research like this paper"---connects the device to the broader economic argument rather than leaving it as a curiosity.

This is the kind of rhetorical move that, when executed well, makes a paper memorable. It gives the reader a concrete, immediate example of the abstract economic force being modeled.

### 4. Is the use of the rhetorical device distracting or overbearing? PASS (not distracting)

The abstract devotes one sentence (out of roughly five) to the device---proportionate and not dominant. The introduction places the device in the final paragraph before the lit review, which is structurally appropriate: it comes after the economic argument is fully laid out, serving as a closing illustration rather than a lead.

The device does not recur in the body of the paper (model, extensions, proofs), which is correct. Repeating it in technical sections would be distracting. Its confinement to the abstract and introduction keeps it in the "framing" register where it belongs.

The only minor concern is the footnote, which restates information already in the main text. Trimming the footnote slightly (e.g., removing "with no human editing applied") would reduce any sense of over-emphasis. But as written, it does not cross into overbearing territory.

## Summary

The self-referential device is well-executed. It appears in both required locations, is framed as an economic illustration rather than a boast, is genuinely compelling, and is contained enough not to distract from the technical contribution.
